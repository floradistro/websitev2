To activate Moonwater vendor in WordPress Admin:

1. Go to: https://api.floradistro.com/wp-admin
2. Navigate to: WooCommerce → Product Vendors → Vendors
3. Find "Moonwater" (moonwater user)
4. Click "Edit"
5. Set Status to "Active"  
6. Click "Save Changes"
